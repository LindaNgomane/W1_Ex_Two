Sphynx

The Sphynx also referred to as the Canadian Sphynx is well known for it's lack of fur. Hairlessness in cats is a naturally occurring genetic mutation, and the Sphynx was developed through selective breeding of these animals, starting in the 1960s.

The Temperament of the Sphynx:

  • intelligent
  • friendly
  • loving
  • energetic
