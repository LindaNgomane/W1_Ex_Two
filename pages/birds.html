Love bird

Love birds are the most well known from the parrot Species. These birds are the most loved type of in Africa. Mostly being admired for their beauty and intelligence. It is called the love bird because of the strong bond with their monogamous mate.

The Temperament of the Love bird

  • Quiet
  • Active
  • Curious
  • Playful
